# Welcome to Slate

*A minimal notes app for people who love to write.*

---

## What is Slate?

Slate is a clean, distraction-free notes app that works everywhere — iOS, Android, and web. Write in markdown, organize with folders, and share anything with a friend in just two taps.

**No clutter. No complexity. Just you and your words.**

---

## Why Slate?

We built Slate because most note apps try to do too much. They drown you in features you'll never use, clutter your thinking with toolbars, and make sharing feel like a chore.

Slate is different:

- **Instant writing** — Open the app, start typing. No setup, no friction.
- **Markdown-native** — Your notes are markdown. Clean, portable, future-proof.
- **Two-tap sharing** — Share a note or folder with anyone in seconds. No account required for them to read.
- **Luxury minimal** — Black and white, beautifully simple. Easy on the eyes.
- **Works everywhere** — Your notes follow you. Phone, tablet, laptop, web.

---

## Who's it for?

- **Writers** who want a clean slate
- **Thinkers** who jot notes, ideas, and drafts
- **Teams** that need to share docs without the bloat
- **Anyone** who values simplicity over feature creep

---

## Key Features

| Feature | What it means for you |
|---|---|
| **Markdown notes** | Write naturally, export anytime |
| **Folders** | Organize notes your way |
| **Simple sharing** | Share via link — recipient can read without signing up |
| **Real-time sync** | Your notes are always up to date, on every device |
| **Turn-based editing** | When sharing, only one person edits at a time — no merge conflicts |
| **Dark mode native** | Black on white, elegant from day one |

## The Stack (for the curious)

Built with:

- **Expo** + **React Native** — Cross-platform mobile + web
- **NativeWind** — Tailwind-style styling
- **Clerk** — Auth (Google + email)
- **Supabase** — Database + realtime

## Get Started

1. **Sign up** — Google or email (takes 30 seconds)
2. **Create a note** — Click + and start writing
3. **Organize** — Drag notes into folders
4. **Share** — Tap share, choose who, done

That's it. Welcome to Slate.
